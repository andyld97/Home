Anleitung: Service erstellen:

1. Skript erstellen:
sudo nano /etc/systemd/system/kestrel-hellomvc.service

----------------------------------------------------------------------              
[Unit]
Description=Home Service Linux

[Service]
WorkingDirectory=/home/andy/Schreibtisch/Service
ExecStart=/usr/bin/dotnet /home/andy/Schreibtisch/Service/Home.Service.Linux.dll
Restart=always
# Restart service after 10 seconds if the dotnet service crashes:
RestartSec=10
SyslogIdentifier=home.service.linux
User=root

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
----------------------------------------------------------------------  

2. Service einrichten (aktivieren & starten)

systemctl enable  kestrel-hellomvc.service # Aktiviert den Service (auch Autostart)
systemctl disable kestrel-hellomvc.service
systemctl restart kestrel-hellomvc.service
systemctl stop kestrel-hellomvc.service
systemctl start kestrel-hellomvc.service
systemctl status kestrel-hellomvc.service
